How it works

Three taps. That's it.

Lcloud finds your PC automatically on your home WiFi. No IP addresses, no pairing codes. No setup beyond installing both apps.

1
📡

Apps find each other

Start the PC app — it broadcasts a presence signal every 2 seconds on your home WiFi. Open Lcloud on your phone and it discovers the PC automatically within seconds. No typing, no codes.

2
🔐

Tap Backup Now

Files stream directly to your PC over HTTPS — the same encryption protecting your online banking. WhatsApp media goes first (most irreplaceable), then newest photos, then videos and documents.

3
📁

Files land in neat folders

Photos sorted into Photos/2026/04/, WhatsApp into WhatsApp/Images/. Organized automatically. Restore any file back to your phone in one tap — its exact original location.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🏠
Fully local — no middleman

Files travel directly from your phone to your PC without touching any server. Not even ours. Your data never leaves your home network. If Lcloud shuts down tomorrow, your backups are unaffected.

🔒
Encrypted transfer

Every file sent over HTTPS with fingerprint-trusted certificates. Zero config, proven on millions of devices by the LocalSend protocol.

🔄
Restore to phone

Browse sessions, pick files, restore to their exact original location. Great for recovering accidentally deleted photos or messages.

🆓
Free. No catches.

No trial. No premium tier. No "upgrade to unlock" banner hiding the useful features. MIT licensed — every line of code is on GitHub. PhotoSync charges €9.99/month for what Lcloud does for free, forever.

💬
Coming in v0.6
Smart priority engine

Lcloud backs up the files most likely to be irreplaceable first. WhatsApp media goes at the top — if your phone is lost or stolen, those chats and voice messages can't be recovered from anywhere else. Then newest photos (most recent aren't cloud-backed yet), then videos. If backup is interrupted at 30%, you still got your most important files. No other free backup app does this.

⚡
Coming in v0.6
Storage threshold trigger

Phone drops below 15% free space? Backup starts automatically in the background and notifies you when done. No app-opening required. Set it and genuinely forget it.

Privacy promise

No data leaves your home. Ever.

Lcloud never connects to any server outside your local network — not even to check for updates. No analytics. No crash reports. No silent data collection.

No account, no email, no tracking

Not now, not ever. We have no way to know you exist — by design.

Open source — read every line

Everything is on GitHub. If it sent data out, you'd see it in the code.

HTTPS even inside your home

End-to-end encrypted over your LAN. Self-signed certificate — no third-party CA that could be compromised.
